The Giants added a veteran receiver Monday morning — but it won’t be a storybook reunion for Odell Beckham Jr.
The Giants instead signed Braxton Berrios after an early-morning workout at the team’s New Jersey facility, according to ESPN’s Adam Schefter.
Berrios could factor more on special teams, where he has had success as a returner.
The Giants intensified their search for a veteran receiver after Gunner Olszewski suffered what is believed to be a torn Achilles.
The Giants brought Berrios, Beckham, JuJu Smith-Schuster and Anthony Miller in for the workout.
Berrios played the first four seasons of his career with the Jets, pulling in a career-high 46 catches in 2021.
The 30-year-old played last season with Texans after two years in Miami.
Berrios in four games with six catches for 37 yards, while returning three punts.
