NewsNation’s Batya Ungar-Sargon — a self-described “MAGA Lefty” who has previously hailed Donald Trump as “the most effective president of my lifetime” — pleaded with the president to help his supporters, as many struggle financially.
“Mr. President, your supporters are hurting,” Ungar-Sargon bluntly said on the air Saturday.
“People who voted for you three times, who would walk over Legos covered in hot coals to vote for you, are broke,” she continued. “Some are skipping meals, others are buying canned chicken in bulk, because that’s all they can afford. The cost of food and gas is just so high.”
Trump voters who support his Iran war had been “willing to pay a little more to make sure our grandkids don’t have to live with the threat of a nuclear-armed terror state” have now “paid all they can,” she said.
Ungar-Sargon pointed to growing frustration among some MAGA supporters, saying savings are at “an all-time low” while credit card delinquency rates continue to climb.
The vast majority of people are “broke,” she said.
“Your supporters need help, Mr. President, and they need it from you. We brought in $200 billion in tariff revenue. Couldn’t some of that be shared with Americans struggling to put food on the table?” Ungar-Sargon asked.
