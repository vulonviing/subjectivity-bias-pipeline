Your new favorite hangout comedy has arrived, and it’s NSFW. Or should we say, Not Suitable for Work!
Comedy legend Mindy Kaling is back with her first new streaming series since Netflix’s Running Point, and with her longtime creative collaborator Charlie Grandy as showrunner, fans won’t want to miss a moment of the delightfully written romance, drama, and comedy.

Ready to meet AJ (Ella Hunt), Abby (Avantika Vandanapu), Davis (Will Angus), Josh (Jack Martin), Kel (Nicholas Duvernay), and Bill (Jay Ellis)? Prepare to be charmed and nostalgic for your twenties!

What Time Does Not Suitable for Work Come Out?
New episodes of Not Suitable for Work premiere on Hulu Tuesdays at 12:00 a.m. ET, which means they’ll be available to stream on Mondays at 9:00 p.m. PT. So Not Suitable for Work, Episode 1 – 3 will be streaming on Tuesday, June 2 at 12:00 a.m. ET / Monday, June 1 at 9:00 p.m. PT.
