Dua Lipa married famous actor Callum Turner in an intimate legal ceremony Sunday at the Old Marylebone Town Hall in London.
Lipa wore a Schiaparelli skirt suit, white gloves and a wide-brimmed white hat. Her stunning yellow bouquet was the perfect color-pop against Turner’s fitted navy blue suit. Their ceremony lasted 30-40 minutes, and close family gathered around them and tossed confetti as the newlyweds exited the registry office and drove away in a black cab, according to Daily Mail.
Lipa and Turner began dating in 2024. Their legal wedding reportedly precedes their lavish, three-day reception plans in Italy.
The lovebirds are planning a big bash in Sicily’s capital Palermo, which is expected to last from Thursday to Saturday, according to Daily Mail.
Their ceremony will unfold at the 17th-century Villa Valguarnera, approximately ten miles away from the capital.
Lipa’s star-studded guest list reportedly includes Sir Elton John, Mark Ronson, Charli XCX, Donatella Versace and Simon Porte Jacquemus. Versace has custom-designed the star’s wedding dress, according to Daily Mail.
No further details have been released about the celebration to follow in the coming days.
