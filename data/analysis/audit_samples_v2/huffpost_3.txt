President Donald Trump over the weekend claimed that most people don’t know how to spell a basic word as he explained his latest insult.
“I take the ‘b’ off because most people don’t know that dumb ends with a ‘b’ but most people don’t know,” the president insisted as he shared why he now refers to Democrats as “Dumocrats.”
“I switch the ‘e’ with a ‘u’ and you have a Dumocrat,” he told daughter-in-law Lara Trump during a [OUTLET] interview, saying he was inspired by watching House Minority Leader Hakeem Jeffries (D-N.Y.).
“He’s a dumb person, and I said, ‘He’s really dumb.’ I said, ‘Wait a minute, he’s a Democrat!’” he said. “So I think it works.”
Trump said he wasn’t sure if the word would get as much traction as some of his other insults.
