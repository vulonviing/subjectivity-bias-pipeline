     - data/analysis/summary_tables.csv — tek bir uzun-format tablo: dimension, key, n, subj_llm_pct, subj_proxy_pct, agreement_pct (dimension ∈ {overall, block, outlet,
     topic_group, block_topic_group, block_topic, outlet_topic}).

     kind (kind ∈ {proxy_missed_subj, proxy_false_subj}); sıralama llm_confidence desc.

     filtreli token sayımı yeterli; abartmadan).
     - Figürler: data/analysis/figures/ altına 3 PNG — (a) outlet SUBJ % bar (LLM vs Proxy yan yana), (b) block × topic_group heatmap, (c) proxy confidence histogramı LLM
